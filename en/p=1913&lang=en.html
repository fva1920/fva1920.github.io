FVA this Thursday at the Introduction day of RWTH's student initiatives

Published: 16.05.2017 by Martin Valley

Beside our own aspirant recruitment we have participated in the “day of student initiatives” for a few semesters now, where we, together with the other initiatives, stand in front of the Super C to introduce ourselfes to new students. During this day one can experience the whole range of student initiatives from debating to gliding, the uni-cinema or radio etc. The next opportunity is this thursday from 10 a.m. stop by, we are looking forward to meet you!
