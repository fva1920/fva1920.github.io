Interested? Join us!

You are a student at the RWTH or FH Aachen and interested in the FVA? No matter what you are studying: You are welcomed!

First steps

1. Text us!

If you´re interested, just send us an e-mail . You can also reach us via Facebook or Instagram .

2. Join our weekly meeting!

We meet every Thursday at 8pm in the lecture hall LU at the RWTH Luft- und Raumfahrttechnik building and discuss whatever is going on. If you are interested in the FVA, feel free to stop by as well. Here you can get to know us and our members better! Feel free to ask us any questions. If you want to join our meeting, just send us an Email!

3. Visit our workshop!

Our workshop located in Würselen is the place, where almost everything takes place! Here, we do research, build our prototypes and maintain our aircrafts. Just contact us, then you can visit our workshop.

4. Accumulate your first working hours!

To start flying with us you need to work for 25 hours a month. There are a variety of tasks, like researching new concepts of Aviation, programming and workshop work or taking pictures and shooting videos.
