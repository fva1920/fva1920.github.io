100 Jahrfeier (100th anniversary celebration)

Published: 29.03.2023 by Vorstand

In the end of May, after three years’ delay caused by the pandemic, the Flugwissenschaftliche Vereinigung Aachen (1920) e.V. will finally celebrate its 100th anniversary. The event will start on Friday with lectures on current topics in aviation. Continuing on Saturday we will first look back at the history of the FVA, followed by presentations on planned future developments. The celebration will end with a festive evening on Saturday.

Schedule:

  • Friday, 26.05.2023 (12-19): Technical lectures on current and future-oriented aviation topics
  • Saturday, 27.05.2023 (10-15:00): Presentation on topics from the FVA’s past as well as future development

Registration for the event can be done either by email to 100@fva.rwth-aachen.de or using the form here

We are looking forward to welcoming you all in Aachen!
