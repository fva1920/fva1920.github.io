Your path into the FVA

Published: 22.09.2025 by Benjamin Bürger

Are you interested in research? Building? Flying? Then the FVA is the right place for you. Come join us on October 16, 2025, at 6:30 p.m. in Lecture Hall LU (Wüllnerstraße 7, 3rd floor) and get to know us! Students from all disciplines are welcome! In a short presentation, you’ll learn everything you need to know about the association, our projects, and, of course, flying. No question will go unanswered. Afterwards, we cordially invite you to our weekly meeting and end the evening together at our local pub.

If you already have any questions, please contact us at info@fva.rwth-aachen.de or on Instagram @fva1920.
